📦 KvK Prep – Items to SAVE (and WHY)

These resources give the highest KvK power spike and should be stockpiled until Prep / Matchmaking is locked.

True Gold – Late-game upgrades, huge power jumps, best value during KvK events
Gems – Universal currency; needed for KvK rushes, events, emergency speedups (hero roulette)
Speedups – Core for Prep Week scoring (build, research, train)
Hero Shards – Massive power gain; save for KvK hero events
Keys (Special Events – e.g. Valentine’s Day) – Best ROI during event windows
Enhancement XP Parts / Mithril – Big power inflation → affects matchmaking
Artisan Vision – Crafting spikes = large hidden power
Charm Designs & Charm Guides – Very high power per item, KvK-weighted
Forgehammers & Gilded Threads – Gear upgrades = strong matchmaking impact
Pet Food / Taming Marks / Nutrient Potions – Pets add a lot of silent power
Growth Manuals & Amulets – Efficient power sources, save for Prep scoring

👉 Rule of thumb:
If it gives permanent power, save it.

🎯 Why TOP 100 Players Must Stay SMALL Until Prep Week

Matchmaking is power-based
Over-spending early inflates kingdom power before KvK
Higher power = harder opponents
Lower power = easier matchup + better odds overall

📉 A “low-powered” kingdom with stockpiled resources is far stronger than it looks.

⚠️ Events Between KvKs – How to Handle Them

Spend minimum only to unlock basic rewards
Skip bad ROI events entirely if needed 
Example: Office Project → low return, high cost
Don’t chase rankings outside KvK unless value is exceptional

🧠 Controlled Spending Policy

If spending must happen:
Only a few players, not everyone
Only players who can compensate later
Timing must be coordinated

🤝 F2P Player Consideration (Important)

Keep small, accessible events for F2P players
Let them score decently and earn rewards
Avoid forcing them into high-cost events
Strong F2P progression = stronger KvK performance overall

✅ Bottom Line

Save big. Spend together. Stay small early. Win later.
