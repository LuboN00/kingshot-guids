🛡️ KINGSHOT – HERO LINEUP GUIDE
(Solo | Rally | Garrison)

════════════════════

📌 FIRST — WHY LINEUPS MATTER
• Different game modes use heroes differently.  
• Solo battles ignore Widgets.  
• Rally & Garrison fights apply Widgets (big stat boosts).  
• Always build lineups based on the *role*, not just raw power.

════════════════════

💥 SOLO ATTACK (PvP | Open World)
🧠 Widgets DON’T WORK here.
• Solo = **raw stats & expedition skills** matter most.
• Use heroes with the best *overall expedition stats*.

Recommended Solo lineup (Example Gen 3):
• **Amadeus** – strong skillset, high base stats  
• **Petra** – great cavalry stats/skills  
• **Jaeger** – best archer choice for raw damage

(Widgets are ignored, so heroes with higher stats > widget bonus here.)

════════════════════

⚔️ RALLY ATTACK
🧠 Widgets ARE APPLIED here — HUGE boost!
• Rally leaders should use heroes with strong **attack/rally widgets**.
• Rally heroes give multiplicative stat boosts based on widgets + skills.

Recommended Rally lineups by generation:
Gen 1:
• Amadeus + Jabel + Saul

Gen 2:
• Amadeus, Hilde, Marlin

Gen 3:
• Amadeus, Petra, Marlin

Gen 4:
• Amadeus, Petra, Rosa

Widgets matter most in rallies — choose heroes whose widgets boost rally stats.
