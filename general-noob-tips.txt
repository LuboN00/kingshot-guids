Otherwise, free to play tips.
1.    Abuse the shops. You can buy speedups for plain ol resources sometimes and small bits of VIP for not a lot of gems. The Mystery Shop has the occasional free item. So make sure to go through every free refresh every day. Don't use gems on refreshes.
2.    Alliance coins Q. They're gods for F2P players. Speedups, advanced teleporters, etc.
3.    Wait for big events to cash things in. For example, Hall of Governors gives you rewards for powering up. So, save your hero shards, speedups, governor gear materials, etc for events like HoG and you'll get rewards for using the stuff you were going to for free up to this point.
4.    Abuse every system you can. Do your dailies, get the free tries of every event and pack, etc. It adds up over time.
5.    Join an active alliance and enable auto join 24/7 or as close to it as possible. More free reward as you join more rallies.
