🔮 Mystic Divination – Quick Guide & Tips (Kingshot)

📅 Event: 2-day event (about every 4 weeks)
🎁 Goal: Pick your Wish Reward, flip cards, and find it using Fortune Tokens.

⚙️ How It Works

Choose 1 of 4 Wish Rewards.
It’s hidden under 1 of 10 cards.
Use Fortune Tokens to flip cards.
Costs go up with each flip:
1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 6 → 8 → 12 → 15 → 20 → 25
(Total = 96 tokens to flip all 10)
You can reset anytime. Reset cost drops the more cards you flip.

🎯 Best Strategy

If you have many tokens:
💎 Flip all 10 cards for max rewards.
If you have few tokens:
⚖️ Stop around 6–8 flips to avoid the expensive 20 & 25-token flips.

Free-to-Play tip:
💰 Save tokens across events and spend them in a big full-flip session later.
🧭 Wish Reward Priorities
🛠️ Forgehammers
🪶 Governor Charm Materials
💎 High-value resource packs or gear mats
💡 Extra Tips

✅ Save your Fortune Tokens — they carry over to next event.
✅ Don’t reset too early (3–4 flips = wasted value).
✅ Watch for milestone rewards (extra prizes based on total tokens used).
✅ Focus on rewards that help your stats, gear, or progression.
