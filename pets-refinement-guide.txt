🦴 KINGSHOT – PETS PRIORITY & REFINEMENT GUIDE

════════════════════

🐾 OVERVIEW
• Pets provide strong passive bonuses to your troops
• Pets can be leveled with food and refined with Taming Marks
• Refinement increases Health & Lethality for Infantry, Cavalry, and Archers
• Pets are a long-term power system — progress slowly and smartly

════════════════════

🎯 PET PRIORITY – F2P PLAYERS
Upgrade pets in this order:

1) Gray Wolf  
2) Bison  
3) Lion  
4) Cheetah  
5) Mighty Bison  
6) Giant Rhino  
7) Grizzly Bear  
8) Moose  
9) Lynx  

Notes:
• If a higher pet isn’t unlocked yet, level the best one you have
• Level Cheetah until Lion unlocks, then switch
• Level Lynx only to Lv20 (enough stamina for daily Pet Adventures)

════════════════════

💰 PET PRIORITY – SPENDERS
Upgrade pets in this order:

1) Mighty Bison  
2) Giant Rhino  
3) Grizzly Bear  
4) Moose  
5) Bison  
6) Lion  
7) Cheetah  
8) Gray Wolf  
9) Lynx  

• High-rarity battle pets give better stat value per level
• Focus combat pets first if spending

════════════════════

🔥 PET REFINEMENT BASICS
• Refinement uses Taming Marks
• Each refine rolls 6 stats:
  - Infantry Health
  - Infantry Lethality
  - Archer Health
  - Archer Lethality
  - Cavalry Health
  - Cavalry Lethality

• Stats roll randomly up or down
• If total power increases → press REPLACE
• If total power decreases → press RETRY
• AUTO refine helps save time

════════════════════

🌈 REFINEMENT RARITY TIERS
Stat rarity upgrades permanently when thresholds are reached:

Gray → Green → Blue → Purple → Gold

• Once a stat upgrades in rarity, it never drops back
• Higher rarity = much stronger bonus
• Gold rolls are rare and expensive
════════════════════

🔧 BEST REFINEMENT STRATEGY
• Use Common Taming Marks for Gray & Green stats
• Switch to Advanced Taming Marks at Blue+
• Purple is a strong realistic target
• Gold is endgame-level luck or investment

════════════════════

📌 PET REFINEMENT PRIORITY
• Refine pets with higher stat ceilings first
• Battle pets benefit most from refinement
• Refinement bonuses apply to ALL troops

════════════════════

🐾 DAILY & LONG-TERM TIPS
• Run Pet Adventures daily for food & materials
• Pet stamina determines how many adventures you can run
• Pets are a slow system — don’t rush refinement early
• Small gains stack up massively over time

════════════════════

🏆 QUICK SUMMARY
✔ F2P: upgrade utility pets first  
✔ Spenders: upgrade battle pets first  
✔ Refinement boosts troop health & lethality  
✔ Purple refinement = excellent progress  
✔ Gold refinement = endgame goal
