📘 KINDSHOT – JOINER HERO MECHANICS GUIDE

════════════════════

🧠 WHAT IS A JOINER HERO?
• When you join a rally, only your **first hero slot** counts.
• That hero becomes your **Joiner Hero**.
• Only the **first skill of that hero** contributes to the rally bonus. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Example:
If your march is [Chenko] [Howard] [Quinn], then:
➡️ Chenko = Joiner Hero
➡️ Only Chenko’s **first skill** adds to rally bonuses. 

════════════════════

💥 HOW MANY JOINER SKILLS COUNT?
• Up to **4 joiner skills** can be added to a rally.
• Only the **first 4 members** who join with a maxed first skill get counted.
• If an early joiner does NOT have their first skill maxed, the next joiner can fill that slot. 

════════════════════

📊 DAMAGE FORMULA (SIMPLIFIED)
Kills = √Troops × (Attack × Lethality) / (Enemy Defense × Enemy Health) × SkillMod

Where:
• **SkillMod** comes from all active skill buffs in the rally.
• Joiner skills boost **DamageUp**, **DefenseUp**, or **OppDamageDown** in SkillMod.
════════════════════

🧩 STACKING JOINER SKILLS – SAME VS DIFFERENT
• If you use 4 of the SAME joiner effect (e.g., all Chenko/Amane damage buffs with same identifier),
  those buffs **just add up**.  
• If you use different identifiers (e.g., DamageUp 101 + DamageUp 102),
  those buffs **multiply**, giving a bigger SkillMod boost. :contentReference

Example:
• 4 identical (e.g., 4 Chenko) → 100% boost  
• 2 Chenko + 2 Amane → ~125% boost  
(Mixing gives more damage than stacking the same.) 

════════════════════

🧠 EFFECT TYPES (Identifiers)
• **DamageUp:** Chenko, Amadeus, Yeonwoo (all ‘101’)
• **DamageUp:** Amane, Margot (with different identifier ‘102’)
• **DefenseUp:** Howard, Quinn, Gordon, Saul, Hilde (various ‘111’, ‘112’, ‘113’)
• **OppDamageDown:** Fahd, Eric (different identifiers ‘201’, ‘202’) 

════════════════════

📍 KEY MECHANIC TAKEAWAYS
✔ Only your first hero’s FIRST skill matters when joining  
✔ Up to 4 joiner skills can be counted  
✔ Heroes with different effect identifiers stack multiplicatively  
✔ Different types of buffs (DamageUp, OppDamageDown, DefenseUp) also stack multiplicatively  
✔ Hero gear **does NOT matter for joiner skill effects** — only the skill itself matters

════════════════════

🎯 BEST PRACTICE FOR JOINERS
• Use a mix of joiner heroes with *different identifiers* to gain multiplicative stack  
• Max out the FIRST skill of your joiner heroes  
• For attack rallies: prioritize DamageUp heroes  
• For garrison: mix DefenseUp with OppDamageDown heroes
