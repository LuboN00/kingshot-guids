Primary objective: Capture and rotate as many turrets as possible, but at least two, and create an active experience for all participants. 

Secondary objective: Dethrone DIV at least once.

Execution: Our offensive rally leaders (ORL) always attack two turrets simultaneously. Ideally, the winning rally includes an additional defensive rally leader (DRL), who is appointed garrison leader immediately upon arrival.

The offensive rally leader then leaves the rally and, upon returning, is immediately available again as an offensive rally leader for further attacks. The only important thing is that the defensive rally leader is not one of the first four joiners.
