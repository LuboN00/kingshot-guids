🔥 Caesars Fury Quick Guide 🔥
✅ Pick the highest difficulty you can handle — it’s locked once chosen.
✅ Scout Caesars (15 stamina) to spawn them fast.
✅ Use Diana to save stamina (8 solo / 20 rally).
✅ Request Help only when you can’t finish a Caesar in 1h.
✅ Help others often — free rewards + unlocks extra help slots.
✅ Save stamina & buffs for high-level or end-event pushes.
✅ Join Alliance Captain fights — any hit = rewards!
❌ Don’t waste stamina on low-level Caesars late in the event.
