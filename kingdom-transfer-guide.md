📜 Kingshot Kingdom Transfer Guide — Tips, Tricks & Costs
🕹️ What is Kingdom Transfer?

Kingdom Transfer is a 7-day recurring event that allows you to migrate your account from your current Kingdom to another active Kingdom within the same group. This is ideal for joining friends, stronger alliances, or better-managed servers.

Transfers typically appear every 4–7 weeks and are announced in-game a few days before starting.

📆 Event Structure

The Kingdom Transfer event runs in 3 phases:

1️⃣ Phase I – Pre-Transfer (3 Days)

Transfer Managers set Power Cap thresholds for their Kingdom.

You can preview available Kingdoms and their requirements.
No actual transfers happen yet — prepare & plan!

2️⃣ Phase II – Invitational Transfer (2 Days)

Transfer Managers can send:

Ordinary Invites (meet Power Cap)
Special Invites (bypass Power Cap) — only for Ordinary Kingdoms
Even invited players must still spend Transfer Passes.

3️⃣ Phase III – Open Transfer (2 Days)

Open to all eligible Governors meeting the Power Cap.
First-come, first-serve: spots fill fast!

🔍 Transfer Requirements

To successfully transfer, you must:

✅ Not be in any Alliance
✅ Not be a King or Transfer Manager
✅ All troops must be safely in your city
✅ Town Center at required level
✅ No ongoing battles
✅ Last transfer was at least 25+ days ago
✅ Have less than 4 characters already in the target Kingdom
✅ Meet the Power Cap limit of the target server

🧠 Kingdom Types

Kingdoms fall into two categories:

🏆 Leading Kingdoms

Stronger servers (based on top 100 players’ Transfer Score)
Lower Power Cap
Can only accept 30 transfers total
Cannot issue Special Invites

📊 Ordinary Kingdoms

Can accept up to 55 free slots + 3 Special Invites (58 total)
Special Invites let high-power players join bypassing Power Caps

💰 Transfer Passes – Costs & How They Work

Every Kingdom transfer costs Transfer Passes. The cost depends on:

🔹 Your Transfer Score (Town Center level + hero stats + gear + pet power)
🔹 Target Kingdom’s strength

💡 The stronger your score vs. the target server, the more passes needed.

📍 Typical range: 1 to 50 Transfer Passes.

🎯 Free-to-play players usually require ~6–12 passes.

🛒 How to Get Transfer Passes

✔ Weekly in the Alliance Shop (costs Alliance Tokens)
✔ Buy packs in the shop during the event
✔ Save them in advance so you’re ready when transfers begin

Recommended: Buy cheaper passes monthly before the event to avoid FOMO purchases.

⚔️ Tips & Tricks

🔹 Plan ahead — don’t wait until the last minute.
🔹 Prepare Power Cap – reduce your Transfer Score if above cap:
• Unequip hero gear
• Reset hero levels
• Avoid applying gear upgrades before the event
🔹 Check Kingdom activity and leadership before choosing your destination.
🔹 Coordinate with your alliance so many move together.
🔹 Even with an invite, you still need Transfer Passes!

📌 What Happens After Transferring

✔ Your city is placed randomly with a 24-hour shield
✔ Chat history resets
✔ Arena points reset
✔ Active event participation resets (but you can join new ones)
✔ Pack purchase limits reset
✔ Unsecured resources may be lost
