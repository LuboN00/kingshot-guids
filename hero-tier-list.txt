🏆 KINGSHOT HERO TIER LIST (COMPACT + EXPLANATION)

This tier list is split by ROLE.
A hero can be very strong in one role and bad in another.
DO NOT use one hero for everything.

────────────────────────
🔥 RALLY (ATTACK)
S: Amadeus | Petra | Marlin | Rosa  
A: Helga  
B: Jabel | Saul | Zoe | Hilde | Eric | Jaeger | Alcar | Margot  
C: Quinn | Howard | Chenko | Gordon  
D: Yeonwoo | Amane | Fahd  

ℹ️ INFO:
• Rally heroes LEAD attacks
• Attack-type widgets matter the most
• Widgets scale multiplicatively → very strong in rallies
• Good rally heroes can be bad defenders

────────────────────────
🛡️ GARRISON (DEFENSE)
S: Jabel | Zoe | Hilde | Eric | Jaeger | Alcar | Margot  
A: Saul  
B: Amadeus | Petra | Marlin | Rosa | Helga  
C: Quinn | Howard | Chenko | Gordon  
D: Yeonwoo | Amane | Fahd  

ℹ️ INFO:
• Garrison heroes DEFEND your base
• Defense-type widgets are key
• Attack heroes drop a lot in defense value
• Pick heroes built for tanking & defense stats

────────────────────────
🤝 JOINER
S: Saul | Hilde  
A: Gordon | Chenko | Amane | Yeonwoo | Amadeus | Margot  
B: Marlin | Petra | Jaeger | Jabel | Helga | Rosa | Alcar | Quinn | Howard | Eric | Fahd  

ℹ️ INFO:
• Joiners DO NOT use widgets
• Only the FIRST hero expedition skill applies
• Stacking the same strong joiner = best results
• S-tier joiners are always the priority

────────────────────────
🐻 BEAR HUNT (JOINERS)
S: Chenko | Amadeus | Yeonwoo | Amane | Margot  
A: Hilde  
B: Marlin | Petra | Rosa  

ℹ️ INFO:
• Bear does NOT deal damage
• Defense buffs are useless here
• Pure damage buffs = best choice
• Some normal joiners are bad for Bear Hunt

────────────────────────
✅ QUICK RULES
• Leading attack? → Rally hero
• Defending base? → Garrison hero
• Joining rally? → Best joiner
• Bear Hunt? → Damage only
