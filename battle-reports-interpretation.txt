🧠 Purpose

Battle Reports help you understand what happened in a match, how your team performed, and where improvements are needed.

⚔️ 1. Overview Section

Result: Win / Loss
Duration: Total time of the battle
Opponent Info: Name, level, and alliance
Power Comparison: Quick glance at strength differences before and after battle

📌 Use this to assess if you were overpowered or if tactics made the difference.

💥 2. Army Performance

Troops Sent vs. Survived: Shows efficiency and losses
Casualties / Wounded / Returned:
High wounded = strong defense or poor formation
High losses = bad matchup or missing buffs
Healable Units: Units you can recover post-battle

📌 Compare your losses with the enemy’s — aim for better survival ratios.

🧩 3. Formation Breakdown

Frontline / Midline / Backline Performance:
Check which troop type absorbed most damage
Identify weak or overexposed lines
Damage Distribution:
Top 3 units or heroes by total damage dealt

📌 Adjust formation next time to protect your key damage dealers.

⚙️ 4. Buffs & Debuffs
Active Skills: Which abilities triggered and how often
Buffs: Attack/Defense boosts that helped you
Debuffs: Effects reducing your stats or efficiency

📌 If you see strong debuffs, consider counter-skills or gear changes.

📊 5. Summary Insights

Victory Reason: Tactical formation, hero synergy, or stat advantage
Improvement Area: Missing buffs, weak units, or poor positioning
Next Step: Rebuild, adjust, and test again
